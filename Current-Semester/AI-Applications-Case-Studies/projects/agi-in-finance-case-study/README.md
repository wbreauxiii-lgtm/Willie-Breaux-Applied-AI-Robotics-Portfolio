# AGI in Finance Case Study

## What This Is

This case study looks at what more capable AI systems could mean for finance. It is partly forward-looking, but not speculative for its own sake. Finance is a useful pressure test because mistakes scale fast, incentives are uneven, and advanced automation changes both opportunity and risk.

## What I Took From It

- more capable AI increases leverage and also increases blast radius
- finance rewards speed, but bad speed is expensive
- future-facing AI discussions are only useful if they stay tied to control, risk, and decision quality

## Concepts In Play

- AGI and advanced AI capability questions
- financial analysis and operations
- strategic risk
- responsible deployment

## Why I Included It

This piece matters because it shows how I think when the technology is moving faster than the rules around it.
