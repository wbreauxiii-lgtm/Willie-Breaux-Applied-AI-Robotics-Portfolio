# Public Sector Procurement Fraud Detection Case Study

## What This Is

This case study focuses on procurement fraud in government and public-sector systems. The use case is straightforward: large process surface area, financial leakage, and a strong need for better oversight. But public-sector AI has to do more than detect patterns. It has to support accountability.

## What I Took From It

- fraud detection in government is tied to governance, not just analytics
- transparency matters more when systems affect public money and public trust
- AI can improve oversight, but weak controls can turn automation into a liability

## Concepts In Play

- fraud analytics
- public-sector AI
- accountability and oversight
- governance risk
- decision support

## Why I Included It

This work sits at the intersection of operations, ethics, and institutional trust. That matters to me because public systems do not get the luxury of being careless.
