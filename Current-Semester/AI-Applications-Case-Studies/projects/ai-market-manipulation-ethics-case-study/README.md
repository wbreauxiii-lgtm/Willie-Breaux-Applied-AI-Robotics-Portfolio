# AI Market Manipulation Ethics Case Study

## What This Is

This case study examines how AI can be used to distort financial markets instead of improving them. That angle matters. Too much AI discussion stays locked on capability and skips misuse, incentives, and downstream harm.

## What I Took From It

- the same systems that create edge can also create abuse
- trust in markets depends on transparency, fairness, and enforcement
- responsible AI work has to account for adversarial use, not just intended use

## Concepts In Play

- financial market behavior
- misuse and abuse risk
- AI ethics
- trust and regulation

## Why I Included It

I included this because serious AI work means looking at failure modes and exploit paths, not just success stories.
